About Us

1. Growide Green Power Advisors is a trusted name in the renewable energy and consulting space. We offer end-to-end advisory services across the Energy, Manufacturing, Corporate, and Services sectors, with a vision to become a global leader in sustainable energy transformation.

2. With a deep-rooted mission to accelerate India’s clean energy journey, we support government utilities, SMEs, and large corporates in building and managing robust renewable power portfolios. Our solutions span the full lifecycle of clean energy development—from strategy to execution.

3. Recognized as one of the leading renewable energy consulting firms in Tamil Nadu, we take pride in our contributions to the growth of wind and solar energy adoption across the state and beyond. We continue to expand our reach, ensuring that organizations of all sizes benefit from clean, efficient, and cost-effective power solutions.

4. Our core offerings include power trading advisory, solar & wind project consulting, I-REC/REC facilitation, BOOT model execution, EPC support, and carbon sustainability advisory. Every service we offer is tailored to meet the unique strategic, technical, and financial needs of our clients.

5. At Growide, we believe in collaborative engagement and client-first execution. We work as extended partners with our clients, bringing in our expertise, agility, and insights to ensure long-term value creation.
