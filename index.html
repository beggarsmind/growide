Advantages

Wind and Solar energy provide a range of strategic and financial advantages:

  • Cost-Effectiveness

    Lower electricity costs than conventional fuels like coal, gas, and diesel.

  • Long-Term Cost Forecast

    Fixed-rate PPAs offer price stability and energy cost predictability.

  • Growing Market

    Driven by rising electricity costs and mandatory Renewable Purchase Obligations (RPOs).

  • Benefit Without Investing

    Minimal upfront investment; pay only for the power consumed.

  • Energy Independence

    Reduces dependence on imported fossil fuels and increases energy autonomy.

  • Sustainability

    Clean, renewable, and inexhaustible energy source for long-term use.

  • Environmental Benefits

    Significantly lowers carbon emissions and reduces overall environmental impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Growide Green Power Advisors offer?+

We offer Power Trading Advisory, Renewable Energy Consulting (Wind & Solar), I-REC/REC facilitation, EPC Solutions, Carbon & Sustainability Advisory, and BOOT model support.

What is a Group Captive Solar Project?+

A Group Captive Project is where a developer and consumers invest in a solar project together, with the consumers holding at least 26% equity and consuming at least 51% of the power.

How can I register for I-REC through Growide?+

You can register your project through Growide using Evident’s platform. We assist with SF-01 and SF-02 form submissions and the full registration process.

Where is Growide's 1 MW solar project located?+

The 1 MW ground-mounted solar project is in Punnam Village, Bhavani Taluk, Erode District, Tamil Nadu. It’s connected to the Thalavaipettai substation.
